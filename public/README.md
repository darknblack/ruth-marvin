# Public Assets

Place your couple's hero image here as `couple-hero.jpg`.

The image will be displayed as a full-screen background in the hero section of the homepage.

**Recommended specifications:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 1920x1080px or larger (will be cropped to fit)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or similar landscape orientation
- File name: `couple-hero.jpg`

The image should be a beautiful photo of the couple that works well as a background with text overlay.
